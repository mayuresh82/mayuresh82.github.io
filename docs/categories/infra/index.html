A primer on GoCast

Orchestration and Service Discovery The term orchestration, in part, refers to the automated deployment of services or applications inside of your infrastructure. The orchestration solution takes care of things like resource allocation, entitlements and the placement of an application or a service on a machine or a group of machines. If the machine your service is deployed on fails for some reason, the orchestration system automatically detects the failure and re-deploys the service according to the service policy.
